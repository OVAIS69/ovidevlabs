Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build a website?

Typically 2-4 weeks depending on the complexity. A simple brochure site is faster, while e-commerce takes a bit longer.

Do I need to provide the text and images?

It helps! But if you don't have them, I can use stock assets and write placeholder copy that guides you on what to say.

Will my website work on mobile phones?

Absolutely. I design "mobile-first", meaning your site will look perfect on everything from an iPhone to a widescreen monitor.

What if I need help after the site is launched?

I offer 30 days of free support after launch to fix any small bugs. After that, we can discuss a maintenance package if you need one.
